This page should describe the research question, the pilot-class context, and the kinds of evidence you expect to collect.

## Working question

How do AI tools alter the practice of research, especially when the work includes programming, mathematical exposition, data analysis, and iterative writing?<Sidenote>This is deliberately broad. Later pages can split this into operational questions about speed, correctness, pedagogy, trust, and reproducibility.</Sidenote>

## Method sketch

- Maintain a session-level research log.
- Preserve prompts, agent outputs, code diffs, and reflections where appropriate.
- Compare outcomes across tools and task types.
- Separate subjective experience from externally checkable artifacts.
